How the "max offer" number is found

1
Fix GDV (£858,181) and every cost that doesn't depend on price (refurb, sale fees).
2
Cash ROI falls as the price rises (pay more → SDLT rises → profit shrinks). So there's exactly one price where ROI = target.
3
Binary-search the offer between £0 and GDV until cash ROI lands on 20%. That price is the max offer.
4
At £579,798: all-in outlay £715,151 · profit £143,030 · ROI 20.0%. ✓
So — "why the max offer?" The Max offer · 20% cash tile is the number that protects the return.
